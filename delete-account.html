Delete your Match Point account

Match Point · Last updated: May 10, 2026

You can delete your Match Point account and all associated data at any time. There are two ways to do this.

Option 1: Delete in-app (fastest)

  1. Open the Match Point app and sign in.
  2. Go to Profile → Settings.
  3. Scroll to Delete Account and tap it.
  4. Confirm. Your account and all associated data are permanently deleted immediately.

Option 2: Request deletion by email

If you can't access the app — for example, you've already uninstalled it or lost access to your sign-in method — email us and we'll delete your account on your behalf.

Email us to delete your account

Include in your email:

  • The email address associated with your Match Point account.
  • A short confirmation that you want your account deleted (e.g. "Please delete my account").

We will process the request within 30 days and reply once it's done.

What gets deleted

When your account is deleted, the following data is permanently removed and cannot be recovered:

  • Your profile (name, photo, NTRP rating, favorite court).
  • Your account credentials and email.
  • All matches you created, accepted, or declined.
  • All conversations and messages you participated in.
  • Your push notification tokens.
  • Any blocks you set or reports you submitted.
  • Any reports submitted about you.

What may be retained

  • Server logs (timestamps, IPs, user-agents) for approximately 30 days for security and debugging.
  • Records we are legally required to keep (we have none at this time).
