Common questions

How do I delete my account?

Open the app, tap your profile, then Settings → Delete account. All your data, matches, and messages are permanently removed.

How do I report or block another player?

From any chat or profile, tap the menu in the top-right and choose Report or Block. Reports are reviewed within 24 hours.

How does skill-level matching work?

Match Point uses the NTRP rating scale (2.0–7.0). You set yours during onboarding, and we surface players within roughly half a point of your level.

My location isn't showing the right courts.

Match Point only shows courts within your search radius. You can widen it from the home screen, or check that location permissions are enabled in iOS Settings.
